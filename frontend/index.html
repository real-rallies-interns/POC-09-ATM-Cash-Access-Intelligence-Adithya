Why This Matters

Cash remains the fail-safe rail for everyday access, emergency withdrawals, and financial resilience. Weak ATM and branch presence can create banking deserts and increase physical travel burden.

Who Controls the Rail

Commercial banks, independent ATM deployers, and regulators shape how physical cash access infrastructure is distributed and maintained across regions.

Data Sources

OpenStreetMap / Overpass API for geospatial infrastructure context.

Structured mock ATM-bank datasets for intelligence simulation.

Metric Basis

Coverage Score = access-point density + area service balance.

Travel Burden = estimated average access time to nearest ATM/bank.

Demographic Risk = simulated service friction indicator for the selected area.
